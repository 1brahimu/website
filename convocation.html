6th convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center

The 6th convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center of the year 2023 continues the tradition within academic institutions of gathering together academicians,students, alumni,benefactors and industry professionals as stakeholders of the university community. It is the time to exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching, research,and professionals development.

This year's convocation has selected the topic"The role of official statistics and big data in the economic trasformation and sustainable development of Tanzania"as the standing point to reflect on the theme of this year's 18 of November African Statistics day.The theme states "Modernizing data ecosystems to accelerate the implementation of African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCTA):The role of offiicial statistics and big data in the economic transfomation and sustainable development of africa"

This year's convocation aims to understand the African Continental Free Trade Area(AfCTA) and the potential role statistics and big data plays in discovering markets and serving customers across africa
